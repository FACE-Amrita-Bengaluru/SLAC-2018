About

What is SLAC?

The one and only 24 hour Hackathon of Amrita School of Engineering SLAC 2018! It is organized by " The Forum For Aspiring Computer Engineers ", Amrita School of Engineering, Bengaluru.

Meet your Peers

Stay Late And Code(SLAC) brings together developers, designers and coders to create, innovate and put them into action.

Platform for your Ideas

Be it a unique application that lets you play 3D tic-tac-toe, or even the next big social network, the hackers build the most fantastic products overnight, anything from useful-to-hilarious products. Join us for this carnival of ideas and walk away with your mind blown!

Choose Your App

Business

Is your app a business concept? Then come sell it to us at SLAC. Business apps change the world, take Flipkart and Zomato as an example.

Utilities

People who're lazy are the best inventors of short-cuts and life hacks. If you belong to this community then show us what you got. I dare you to live your life without ES File Explorer, Google Translate or AppLock

Games

Everyone loves playing games. Beating the high score is always a thriller. Here's a chance for you to create your own. Are you game?
